About

HR that actually helps.

Kenna Consulting was founded by Danica Sun Kenna on a simple idea: companies under 500 people deserve serious HR expertise without the overhead of a full executive team. Most are stuck between scrappy DIY HR and the cost of a Chief People Officer — and the result is usually compliance gaps, burned-out managers, and turnover nobody can explain.

The work is hands-on and pragmatic. Danica writes the handbook, sits in on the tough conversations, runs the rewards analysis, and coaches your managers herself. No subcontractors, no slide-deck theater.

If you want a thinking partner who'll tell you what she actually thinks — and then help you do it — we'll get along.
